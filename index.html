unified.diff

For too long, Cardiff's software development community has been fragmented by language, discipline, and platform.

unified.diff is a monthly meet-up for anybody involved or interested in the field of software development.

We hold tech talks, and trade war stories.

  • Upcoming Meetup
  • Previous Events & Talk Videos

November's #udiff saw us temporarily relocate to Cardiff's Promised Land, where we had some good 'ol live coding on CouchDB from @mark_j_s, and a fascinating presentation on the Raspberry Pi from @douggore!

December will be a slightly different affair, as our quiz master @handybitesize takes us through various rounds of coding, hardware, IT and tech questions!

We've got the BIG O'NEILLS for this one, so we REALLY hope to see you all there!
